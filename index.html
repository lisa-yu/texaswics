Welcome to Texas WiCS!

Women in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a network and community of women in Computer Science. Our mission is to encourage and support women in computing through outreach, professional development, academic initiatives, and social events.

Mission: To cultivate a welcoming, supportive environment for women in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin.

A Look: What does WiCS do?



Mentorship

receive advice and support from mentors through our mentorship programs such as Nell Dale Mentorship Program, Industry Mentorship, and Faculty Lunches


Academic

study nights and officer hours are available the night before major CS exams


Corporate Events

meet and learn from industry professionals from WiCS and FoCS sponsors through tech talks, workshops, and social events


Social

provides opportunites to spend time with other WiCS members outside of class


Outreach

connect with younger girls through events like ExploreUT, Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day & local school volunteering to encourage them to pursue technology


Makers Tutorials

regular workshops to teach hardware concepts with a hands-on approach
